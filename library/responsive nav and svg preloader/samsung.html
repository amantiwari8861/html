Samsung Mobile -FAQ

1. Which are the best Samsung phones under Rs 20,000?

Samsung has a plethora of phones under Rs 20,000 with great specifications. Among these, Samsung Galaxy On Max, J7 Max and J7 Prime are some of the most popular mobiles. Samsung On Max has a 5.7 inch Full HD display screen, is based on the Android (7.0 ) Nougat OS and is powered by the MediaTek MTK6757V/WL 2.39GHz, 1.69GHz, Octa Core Processo. The camera of this phone is pretty impressive with a 13MP Rear Camera and a 13MP Front Camera. Similarly, the J7 Max and J7 Prime also have similar specifications.

2. Which is the best Samsung camera phone ? Among the recently released Samsung mobile phones Samsung S8 and S8 Plus are two of the best ones for photography. The camera of this smartphone is designed with great precision and craftsmanship that the photos look no less than real. The rear camera of Samsung Galaxy S8 & S8 Plus have a resolution of 12MP, an aperture of f/1.7, focal length of 26mm and is packed with features like phase detection auto-focus, OIS (Optical Image Stabilization) and an LED flash. The sensor size is 1/2.5" and has a pixel size of 1.4 µm.Selfies clicked with the Samsung S8 &S8 Plus cameras are bound to make you look all the more beautiful with specifications like a resolution of 8MP, f/1.7, autofocus and more.

3. What is Samsung Pay ? What are it's features? Samsung Pay is a mobile payment and digital wallet service which enables users to make payments easily. It works at almost all stores and supports contactless payments using NFC (Near-field communication) and even the ones that use the old magnetic-stripe point-of-sale terminals. Samsung Pay works with the Galaxy S8, S8 Plus, S7, S7 Edge, S6 Edge+, S6, S6 Edge, S6 Active and Galaxy Note 5. It also works with Gear S2 and Gear S3. You can make payments using this digital wallet by following three simple steps- swipe up, authenticate and pay.

4. How to upgrade my Samsung phone to the latest Android version? You can upgrade your Samsung mobile to the latest Android version through different ways. One way is to update it Over the Air (OIA). Steps to follow to update Android OTA: 1. Go to your device settings and click on the gear shaped icon. 2. Scroll down and tap 'About Device'. 3. Tap on 'Software Update' or 'System Firmware Update' (depending on the version of Android you are running). 4. Tap 'Check for updates' Your device will search for updates that are compatible with your device. 5. Click on 'Update' which will show up if there is an update available. 6. Now click on 'Install', which may also read as '"Reboot and Install' or 'Install System Software" which will now start the installation process.

5. How to use Bixby- Samsung's Smart Assistant? Bixby is the name of the smart voice assistant from Samsung. It was first released in Samsung S8 and S8 Plus. It is similar to Siri and Google Assistant but can do a number of things that these cannot. For instance Bixby can toggle Power Saving, Performance mode, Blue light filter, Smart View, Do not disturb and more. You just need to press and hold the button on the left edge of the screen, below the volume controls, to Bixby wake up. You can use voice commands or type in one. To open Bixby Voice, you just need to press and hold the Bixby key and speak to Bixby. Say "Hi Bixby" to wake up Bixby Voice. In case you dont prefer to speak a command to this smart assistant, you can open 'Bixby Voice' in full screen mode and tap on 'Search Commands'. You can alternatively press and hold the Bixby Voice icon and drag it over the keyboard icon. Once the keyboard opens you can type in your own commands.

7. Which are some of the best 4G VoLTE Samsung mobiles? There are numerous options under Samsung mobiles that offer 4G VoLTE (Voice Over LTE) services. Samsung J7 Max is one of the best among the smartphones that offer this type of connectivity.

This app provides everything you need including all kinds of services as well as entertainment. It has music, videos, gaming, services like ordering food, booking a cab, among others, and all other kinds of services.
